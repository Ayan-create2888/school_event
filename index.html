Welcome to our school events page

Our school organizes many events every year to help students learn and grow. These events include sports day, games, and science fairs.

Sports Day

Sports Day is one of the most exciting events in our school. Students participate in running, jumping, and team games.

Games Played in Our School

  • Cricket
  • Football
  • Kho-Kho
  • Badminton

Science Fair

The science fair helps students show their creativity. Students make models and explain scientific ideas.

Steps of Science Fair

  1. Selection of topic
  2. Preparation of model
  3. Explanation by students
  4. Judgement and results
